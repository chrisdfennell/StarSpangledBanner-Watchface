Star Spangled Banner — 4th of July Face

(Alternates: "Star Spangled Banner — Fireworks Watch Face" /
 "Star Spangled Banner: Fireworks & Flag")


------------------------------------------------------------------------
FIELD: Description (Max 4000 characters)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Star Spangled Banner is a premium digital watch face that brings Independence Day to your wrist all year round — and celebrates America's 250th in 2026. Fireworks rise from an open field and burst overhead, a waving American flag stands in the breeze, fireflies drift through the summer night, and a distant tree line rings the horizon, all procedurally rendered. Large outlined time and clean complications keep you connected through the celebration.

FEATURES & DETAILS
• FIREWORKS SHOW: Rockets streak up from the field and burst into expanding, drooping, fading showers of color overhead — several shells at once, all evening and night. A true 4th-of-July sky.
• LIVING SUMMER SKY: A procedural backdrop that shifts by time of day — warm dawn, bright midday blue, a golden hour, a fiery sunset, then a deep starry night where the fireworks shine.
• OPEN FIELD & TREE LINE: An open, rolling green field ringed by a silhouetted distant forest edge — the "standing in a wide-open field" look — with a waving Stars-and-Stripes flag as its centerpiece.
• REAL SUN ARC: A warm summer sun and a silver moon arc across day and night, driven by the REAL sunrise/sunset for your location and date — sky, day/night, and fireworks all follow the true sun times.
• FIREFLIES AT NIGHT: Soft yellow-green glints drift and blink over the field once the sun goes down.
• AMERICA'S 250TH: A golden "1776 ★ 2026" banner marks the Semiquincentennial — 250 years of independence. Toggle it off any time.
• GRAND FINALE MODE: Flip one switch to crank up the show — denser, faster fireworks day and night, patriotic red-white-blue bunting draped across the top, a twinkling flag finial, and a biplane that sweeps past trailing a star-spangled pennant.
• FIELD CRITTERS: Occasional visitors cross the scene — one at a time, every ~40 seconds. By day: a soaring bald eagle, a grazing deer, a hopping cottontail, a fluttering butterfly. By night: an owl, a red fox, a raccoon, and a darting bat. One switch turns them on or off.
• SPINNING STAR SECONDS: A five-pointed star second indicator orbiting the perimeter.
• CONFIGURABLE COMPLICATIONS: Set the bottom-left and bottom-right to Heart Rate (live BPM), Body Battery, Device Battery (live fill bar), Steps, Calories, or Off, each with a matching icon. Heart rate samples at most once every ~10 seconds to spare the battery. A red-white-blue steps progress bar with a numeric count sits in the center.
• HIGH-CONTRAST OUTLINES: All texts carry a crisp black outline for readability against the dynamic backdrops.

BUILT FOR YOUR DISPLAY
• AMOLED (Fenix 8, Forerunner 965/970, Venu, epix, Instinct 3 AMOLED, and more): smooth gradient skies, glowing bursts, and active background illumination.
• MIP / Solar (Fenix 7, Fenix 8 Solar, Forerunner 255/955, Instinct, and more): high-contrast flat fills tuned for sunlight readability.
Fonts are baked for every resolution, so it looks right from 166px Instinct panels up to 454px flagships.

ALWAYS-ON FRIENDLY
On AMOLED, the always-on display switches to a burn-in-safe variant — dim grey time/date, thin outline battery metrics and steps progress, no fills or animation, and lit pixels shifted each minute. MIP panels always show the full face.

SETTINGS
• Show Date — toggle the date line.
• Fireworks — toggle the rising-and-bursting fireworks show.
• America's 250th — toggle the golden "1776 ★ 2026" Semiquincentennial banner.
• Grand Finale — denser fireworks, bunting, a spotlit flag, and the biplane flyover.
• Field Critters — toggle the occasional crossing visitors (eagle, deer, rabbit, butterfly, owl, fox, raccoon, bat).
• Step Goal Override — steps for a full progress bar; 0 uses the watch's own step goal.
• Bottom-Left Complication — Off / Heart Rate / Body Battery / Device Battery / Steps / Calories.
• Bottom-Right Complication — same options.

PRIVACY
Star Spangled Banner uses only the SensorHistory permission (to read Body Battery). Heart rate and last-known location are read on-device to show your BPM and compute local sunrise/sunset; no data is collected or transmitted off your watch.

SUPPORTED DEVICES
Every Connect IQ 4.0+ round watch, including: Forerunner 165, 255/255S, 265/265S, 570, 955, 965, 970; fenix 7 / 7S / 7X (incl. Pro), fenix 8 (AMOLED & Solar), fenix E; epix 2 / epix Pro (42/47/51mm); enduro 3; Venu 2 / 2S / 2 Plus, Venu 3 / 3S, Venu 4; Vivoactive 5 / 6; Instinct 3 (AMOLED & Solar), Instinct E, Instinct Crossover; Approach S50 / S70; Descent G2 / Mk3; D2 Air X10 / Mach 1 / Mach 2; MARQ 2 / Aviator. (Square Venu Sq 2 and Venu X1 are not supported.)

Equip Star Spangled Banner, and let the rockets fly.
