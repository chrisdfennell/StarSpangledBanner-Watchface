Star Spangled Banner — 4th of July Face

(Alternates: "Star Spangled Banner — Fireworks Watch Face" /
 "Star Spangled Banner: Fireworks & Flag")


------------------------------------------------------------------------
FIELD: Description (Max 4000 characters)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Star Spangled Banner is a premium digital watch face that brings Independence Day to your wrist all year round — and celebrates America's 250th in 2026. Fireworks rise from an open field and burst overhead, a waving American flag stands in the breeze, fireflies drift through the summer night, and a distant tree line rings the horizon, all procedurally rendered. Large outlined time and clean complications keep you connected through the celebration.

FEATURES & DETAILS
• FIREWORKS SHOW: Rockets streak up from the field and burst into expanding, fading showers of color — several at once, all evening and night.
• LIVING SUMMER SKY: A backdrop that shifts by time of day — warm dawn, bright midday blue, golden hour, a fiery sunset, then a deep starry night where the fireworks shine.
• OPEN FIELD & TREE LINE: A rolling green field ringed by a distant forest edge — that wide-open-field look — with a waving Stars-and-Stripes flag as its centerpiece.
• REAL SUN ARC: A summer sun and silver moon arc across the sky, driven by the REAL sunrise/sunset for your location and date.
• FIREFLIES AT NIGHT: Soft yellow-green glints drift and blink over the field after dark.
• AMERICA'S 250TH: A golden "1776-2026" banner marks the Semiquincentennial — 250 years of independence. Toggle it any time.
• GRAND FINALE MODE: One switch cranks up the show — denser fireworks day and night, red-white-blue bunting across the top, a twinkling flag, and a biplane trailing a star-spangled pennant.
• FIELD CRITTERS: Occasional visitors cross the scene, one at a time. By day: a bald eagle, deer, cottontail, butterfly. By night: an owl, red fox, raccoon, bat.
• SPINNING STAR SECONDS: A five-pointed star orbits the perimeter as the seconds tick.
• CONFIGURABLE COMPLICATIONS: Set the bottom-left and bottom-right to Heart Rate, Body Battery, Device Battery (live fill), Steps, Calories, or Off — each with a matching icon. A red-white-blue steps bar with a count sits in the center.
• HIGH-CONTRAST OUTLINES: All text carries a crisp black outline for readability against the moving backdrop.

BUILT FOR YOUR DISPLAY
Looks right on both AMOLED (smooth gradient skies, glowing bursts) and MIP/Solar (high-contrast flat fills tuned for sunlight), with fonts baked for every resolution from 166px Instinct panels up to 454px flagships.

ALWAYS-ON FRIENDLY
On AMOLED the always-on display switches to a burn-in-safe variant — dim time/date, thin outline metrics, no fills or animation, pixels shifted each minute. MIP panels always show the full face.

SETTINGS
Show Date • Fireworks • America's 250th • Grand Finale • Field Critters • Step Goal Override • Bottom-Left & Bottom-Right Complications (Off / Heart Rate / Body Battery / Device Battery / Steps / Calories).

PRIVACY
Uses only the SensorHistory permission (Body Battery). Heart rate and last-known location are read on-device to show your BPM and compute local sunrise/sunset; no data is collected or transmitted off your watch.

SUPPORTED DEVICES
Every Connect IQ 4.0+ round watch — Forerunner (165, 255/S, 265/S, 570, 955, 965, 970), fenix 7/7S/7X & 8 (AMOLED & Solar) & E, epix 2 / Pro, enduro 3, Venu 2/3 & 4, Vivoactive 5/6, Instinct 3 / E / Crossover, plus Approach, Descent, D2, and MARQ models. (Square panels — Venu Sq 2, Venu X1 — are not supported.)

Equip Star Spangled Banner, and let the rockets fly.
